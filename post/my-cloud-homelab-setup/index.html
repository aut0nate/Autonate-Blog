Why a VPS?

The homelab subreddit is full of impressive setups, dedicated racks, enterprise gear, and serious investment. I enjoy seeing those, but for me, a low-cost VPS was the right middle ground.

  • No worries about hardware failures or power bills.

After looking around, I landed on Contabo. Their VPS plans are very affordable and, so far, rock-solid in performance. I started with a fresh Ubuntu install and built my cloud lab from there.

For services I want to access outside my Tailnet, I combine Nginx Proxy Manager (NPM) with Cloudflare Tunnels.
